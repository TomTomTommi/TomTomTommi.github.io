Junpeng Jing

I am a Ph.D. student at Imperial College London, supervised by Professor Krystian Mikolajczyk. I do research in computer vision, including 3D reconstruction from stereo and 3D learning/understanding.

Before starting my Ph.D., I completed my Master at Beihang University, where I was co-supervised by Professor Zhenyu Guan, Mai Xu and Xin Deng. I also completed my Bachelor at Beihang University.
